How it works

It is as simple as browsing and adding the groceries to your cart then checking out. When you order they will be delivered that same day.

  1. Click on Shop and fill your web shopping cart.
  2. Walk through the secure payment process.
  3. Select what day to deliver or set up recurring deliveries. (same day delivery available!)
  4. Wait tell you get a text or an email your groceries are on your front door. (Don't worry we will place your cool items in a cooler with ice)
  5. It's really that simple!

Where does it work?

Right now we just deliver in Corvallis. We plan to expand across the Northwest soon.
